About Us

Rest Church is a vibrant community dedicated to making disciples who love God, love people, and impact the world. Our foundation is the unwavering belief in the teachings of Jesus Christ, and we strive to create an environment where individuals can grow in their faith and find a true sense of belonging. We are more than just a church; we are a family united by a shared purpose and a deep desire to see lives transformed through the power of the Gospel.

Our vision is to be a beacon of hope and a place of rest for all who seek spiritual nourishment. We are committed to providing meaningful worship experiences, engaging biblical teachings, and opportunities for genuine fellowship. At Rest Church, we believe in the importance of community and the power of working together to make a positive difference in our city and beyond. Join us as we journey together in faith, hope, and love, and discover the joy of a life dedicated to Christ.
