About Rest Church

Discover our heart, our mission, and what makes our community special

Rest Church is more than a gathering place – we're a vibrant community dedicated to making disciples who love God, love people, and impact the world. Our foundation is built on the unwavering belief in the teachings of Jesus Christ, and we strive to create an environment where individuals can grow in their faith and find a true sense of belonging.

We believe in the power of authentic community, meaningful worship, and practical biblical teaching that transforms lives. Whether you're taking your first steps in faith or have been walking with Jesus for years, you'll find a place to grow, serve, and make a difference.

Our vision is to be a beacon of hope and a place of rest for all who seek spiritual nourishment. Join us as we journey together in faith, hope, and love, discovering the joy of a life dedicated to Christ.

Biblical Truth

We believe the Bible is God's Word and the foundation for all we believe and practice.

Authentic Community
